21:T17e5,<h1 id="introduction" class="mb-4">Introduction</h1><p class="mb-4">Server log analysis is one of the most powerful yet underutilized techniques in SEO. By examining your server logs, you can gain unprecedented insights into how search engines crawl your site, identify technical issues, and optimize your crawl budget.</p><h2 id="what-are-server-logs" class="mb-4">What Are Server Logs?</h2><p class="mb-4">Server logs are files that record every request made to your web server. They contain valuable information including:</p><li class="ml-8 mb-4">IP addresses of visitors and bots</li><li class="ml-8 mb-4">Requested URLs</li><li class="ml-8 mb-4">HTTP status codes</li><li class="ml-8 mb-4">User agents</li><li class="ml-8 mb-4">Timestamps</li><li class="ml-8 mb-4">Response sizes</li><h2 id="why-server-log-analysis-matters-for-seo" class="mb-4">Why Server Log Analysis Matters for SEO</h2><h3 id="1-crawl-budget-optimization" class="mb-4">1. Crawl Budget Optimization</h3><p class="mb-4">Understanding how search engine bots crawl your site helps you optimize your crawl budget. You can identify:</p><li class="ml-8 mb-4">Pages that are being crawled too frequently</li><li class="ml-8 mb-4">Important pages that aren't being crawled enough</li><li class="ml-8 mb-4">Crawl errors that waste budget</li><h3 id="2-technical-seo-issues" class="mb-4">2.
